# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA / Test Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Test Leads ensure product quality meets acceptance criteria through comprehensive test strategy, automation, and validation. They collaborate with developers on test coverage and manage quality gates before release.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain test strategy and quality standards
- Validate acceptance criteria for each backlog item
- Collaborate with developers on test automation and coverage
- Manage quality gates (unit tests, integration tests, smoke tests)
- Conduct manual QA for feature acceptance when needed
- Report quality metrics and risks

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Enable confident releases through comprehensive testing
- Improve test coverage and reduce manual testing overhead

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and refinement (acceptance criteria review)
- Daily standups (test progress and blockers)
- PR reviews (test coverage validation)
- Release checklists and post-release verification

---

## Technical Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads or Architects guide technical design decisions, system integration, and long-term architecture. They identify technical risks, mentor developers, and ensure scalability and maintainability.

### Responsibilities
- Lead design reviews and architecture decisions
- Identify and propose solutions for technical risks
- Mentor developers and conduct code reviews
- Ensure system integration and component dependencies are managed
- Document technical decisions and design rationale
- Collaborate with product and project leads on feasibility and trade-offs

### Goals
- Deliver scalable, maintainable solutions
- Reduce technical debt and rework
- Build team capability through mentoring
- Minimize integration issues and rework

### Typical Communication
- Design discussions and technical kickoffs
- Code reviews and architecture reviews
- Risk registers (technical risk mitigation)
- Knowledge sharing and documentation

---

## Stakeholder / Business Sponsor

### Role Summary
Business owners or sponsors have decision authority over scope, priorities, and funding. They provide business context, approve major decisions, and receive regular status updates.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and success metrics
- Approve scope and prioritization decisions
- Allocate budget and resources
- Receive and approve status updates and major milestones
- Make trade-off decisions (scope, timeline, resources)
- Escalate business-critical issues and risks
- Champion the project internally and externally

### Goals
- Maximize business value and ROI
- Ensure project alignment with business priorities
- Remove blockers and resource constraints
- Communicate progress to leadership and customers

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates and milestone reviews
- Kickoff and planning approval meetings
- Escalation and decision meetings (as needed)
- Release announcements and launch coordination

---

## Security / Compliance Officer

### Role Summary
Security and Compliance Officers ensure security and regulatory compliance throughout project delivery. They review designs and code for security implications and participate in incident response.

### Responsibilities
- Define security and compliance requirements for the project
- Review designs and code for security vulnerabilities
- Conduct or coordinate security testing and scanning
- Ensure compliance with applicable regulations and standards
- Participate in risk assessments and incident response
- Provide security guidance and training to team members
- Maintain security documentation and audit trails

### Goals
- Prevent security breaches and data loss
- Ensure compliance and reduce regulatory risk
- Build a security-aware culture
- Enable rapid, secure incident response

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews (security architecture)
- Release checklist (security scanning and approvals)
- Risk registers (security threats and mitigations)
- Incident response playbook and post-mortems
- Compliance audits and documentation reviews

---

## DevOps / Platform Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps or Platform Engineers manage deployment pipelines, infrastructure, and observability. They support release readiness, enable fast iteration, and ensure production reliability.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure, monitoring, and alerting
- Support deployment planning and execution
- Ensure rollback and disaster recovery capabilities
- Collaborate with developers on operational requirements
- Troubleshoot production issues and performance problems
- Document operational runbooks and playbooks

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable deployments
- Maximize system uptime and performance
- Reduce deployment risk and recovery time
- Support developer velocity through self-service tooling

### Typical Communication
- Release planning and deployment readiness checklists
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Infrastructure and operational requirement reviews
- Monitoring dashboards and alerts
- CI/CD pipeline improvements and automation
